Can LoRa be used for a mars rover?

During my studies in Uni we were asked to design and build a small scale mars rover. The part that I invested most of my time into was commuincation, I wanted to check if LoRa(Long Range) could be used to commuincate with a mars rover. This led me to quite a side quest, which I summarised here in a report.
